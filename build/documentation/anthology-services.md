# Anthology Publishing Platform Services

## Ownership System

The Anthology Publishing Platform implements a transparent and flexible ownership system designed to protect all stakeholders while facilitating creative collaboration.

### Contributor Ownership

* **Original Content Rights**: Contributors retain copyright ownership of their original submissions
* **Tiered Ownership**: Ownership percentage allocated based on word count contribution, editorial role, and contractual agreements
* **Digital Rights Management**: Integrated DRM tools to protect author intellectual property
* **Attribution Requirements**: Mandatory attribution for all contributors in appropriate prominence

### Anthology Owner Rights

* **Collection Rights**: Anthology creators hold rights to the collection as a unified work
* **Curation Authority**: Final decision-making power on inclusion, arrangement, and presentation
* **Brand Management**: Control over anthology branding, marketing materials, and related assets
* **Revenue Distribution Authority**: Administration of payment structures according to established agreements

### Platform Ownership Protections

* **Dispute Resolution System**: Structured mediation and arbitration processes
* **Ownership Documentation**: Automated creation of ownership certificates and contracts
* **Rights Reversion**: Clear timelines and conditions for rights reversion to contributors
* **Succession Planning**: Protocols for ownership transfer in case of incapacitation or dissolution

## Publishing Rights

Publishing rights within the platform are designed to maximize distribution opportunities while protecting all contributors' interests.

### Licensing Options

* **Exclusive Publishing Window**: Configurable exclusivity periods for initial publication
* **Non-Exclusive Rights**: Standard terms for ongoing anthology publication after exclusivity period
* **Creative Commons Options**: Support for various CC license implementations
* **Commercial Usage Rights**: Clearly defined terms for commercial exploitation of anthology content

### Rights Management

* **Territorial Rights**: Granular control over publication territories and languages
* **Digital vs. Print Rights**: Separate management of electronic and physical publication rights
* **Derivative Works**: Clear guidelines on adaptation, translation, and other derivative creations
* **Audio Rights**: Specific provisions for audiobook and audio adaptation licensing

### Rights Enforcement

* **Automated Scanning**: Digital content scanning to detect unauthorized usage
* **DMCA Implementation**: Streamlined processes for copyright violation reporting
* **Legal Support Network**: Access to specialized publishing attorneys at preferential rates
* **Rights Violation Documentation**: Systematic tracking of infringements for potential legal action
